# NanoBanana Logo Prompts

Use these prompts with `GEMINI_BASE_URL=http://zx2.52youxi.cc:3000` and a key that has a working `gemini-3-pro-image-preview` channel.

## A. Little Observation Window

Create a square logo concept sheet for a friendly macOS product named "观察者 / Observer".
The core mark is a little rounded observation window with a single teal observation dot inside.
Show one polished large app icon and five small status variants: normal, scanning, warning, critical, paused/offline.
The design should be playful but professional, vector-friendly, readable at 16px menu bar size, with graphite outlines, white/teal base, blue scanning accent, amber warning, coral red critical, and gray paused/offline.
Avoid realistic eyes, surveillance cameras, robots, circuit boards, purple-blue AI gradients, and text inside icons.

## B. Radar Eye

Create a square logo concept sheet for a friendly macOS product named "观察者 / Observer".
The core mark is an abstract radar-eye hybrid: circular lens, central teal dot, one scan arc, and a small signal point.
Show one polished large app icon and five small status variants: normal, scanning, warning, critical, paused/offline.

Use graphite, white, teal, sky blue, amber, coral red, and neutral gray.
Avoid realistic eyes, security camera mood, robots, circuit boards, and one-note purple/blue AI styling.

## C. Tiny Observer Face

Show one polished large app icon and five small status variants: normal, scanning, warning, critical, paused/offline.
